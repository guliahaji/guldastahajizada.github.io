About Me

With over five years of experience in business analytics, I specialize in data-driven strategy, process optimization, and project management. My professional journey has given me the privilege to collaborate with top consulting firms like McKinsey & Company, helping organizations streamline operations and drive impactful transformation initiatives.

Currently, I am a Data Analyst at The University of Tulsa, where I work on university-wide data projects, focusing on analytics and reporting to support strategic fundraising goals. I also bring expertise from my recent internship at Pitney Bowes, where I developed predictive models and optimized resources to enhance operational efficiency.

I am skilled in a variety of advanced analytics tools, including SQL, Python, and Power BI, enabling me to turn complex data into actionable insights. My approach to analytics centers around creating accessible data solutions, building dashboards, and fostering collaboration to deliver measurable business outcomes.

In addition to my hands-on technical skills, I hold a Master of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA) from The University of Tulsa and a dual MBA from La Sapienza University and Baku Engineering University, with a solid foundation in strategic planning and big data analytics.
